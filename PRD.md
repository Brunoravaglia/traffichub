# Product Requirements (Design-Safe Summary)

## Product
Vurp is a platform for paid media managers to centralize operations, dashboards, and reporting.

## Non-negotiable Functional Expectations
- Existing routes and flows must remain functional.
- Public content pages keep SEO-relevant structure.
- Utilities cards must navigate correctly to their respective tools.
- Auth CTAs must consistently drive account creation/login paths.

## Design Scope Constraints
- Visual improvements must not alter business logic.
- No backend/data model/API changes in design passes.
- Any flow change requiring logic must be flagged separately.
